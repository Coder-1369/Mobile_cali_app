How the timers work

Rest timer is a simple count-up or preset countdown. It is for breaks between sets.

Hold timer records the exact time you are in position. Tap Start when you go up, Stop when you come down.

Advanced stopwatch shows Lap (last segment), Split (time since the first lap), and Total time. Use Lap to mark each set.
