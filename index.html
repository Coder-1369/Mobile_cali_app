Planche Day
Session structure · ~50 min
4 × Hold — Advanced tuck (band then no-band)
3 × Push — Tuck PU + Pseudo push-up
2 × Press — L-sit press to tuck
Rest 2–3 min between sets. Film at least 1 set.
Advanced Tuck Planche (Band) Hold
2 sets · 6–8 sec
Rest 2 min
Start band-assisted. Exaggerate protraction — 110%. Knees true 90°. Shoulders down, never at ears.
Advanced Tuck Planche Hold
2 sets · 4–6 sec
Rest 3 min
Stop the rep when protraction breaks. 4 clean seconds beats 8 sloppy ones every time.
Tuck Planche Push-Up Push
2 sets · 2–3 reps + 3s hold
Press to top, hold 3 sec. The hold is the training stimulus — don't skip it.
Pseudo Push-Up (Max Lean) Push
1 set · 6–8 reps
Hands 45° out, maximum lean. 2 sec eccentric. Builds anterior delt reserve.
L-sit Press to Tuck Planche Press
2 sets · 2–3 reps
Rest 2–3 min
Most skipped exercise. Most important for advanced tuck. Use feet assist to learn the path first.
Front Lever Day
Session structure · ~45 min
4 × Hold — Adv tuck (one leg each side, then bilateral)
3 × Pull — Tuck FL rows + Straight-arm pull-downs
Rest 2–3 min between sets. Film at least 1 set.
Adv Tuck FL · One Leg (R + L) Hold
2 sets each side · 5–8 sec
Right leg extended, left tucked. Build both sides to 8 sec before going bilateral. Scap depressed + retracted throughout.
Adv Tuck FL · Both Legs Hold
2 sets · 4–6 sec
Rest 3 min
Both knees at 90°. Push bar backward hard. Back near horizontal. Stop before form breaks.
Tuck Front Lever Row Pull
3 sets · 4–5 reps + 3s hold
From tuck FL, pull chest to bar, extend back to straight-arm. Top hold = key stimulus.
Straight-Arm Pull-Down Pull
2 sets · 10–12 reps
Cable or band. Trains shoulder extension — the exact FL motor pattern. Keep this in every FL session.
Deload every 4–6 weeks
Drop both skills back one level, cut sets in half. Keep wrist prep and hollow body work. This is where your adaptations consolidate — don't skip it.
